I’m working on redesigning Vai Technology’s website. They are a boutique consulting firm that builds artificial intelligence and machine learning systems. The new website will launch near the beginning of September.

I started playing around with Rasberry Pi (it’s a tiny computer). My first project was the /fish page. The Rasberry Pi reads the temperature from a sensor in the fish tank. Then it sends that data to the /fish page, updating once a minute.

Up next, I’m going to automate the light schedule. Right now the fish wake up and fall asleep along with my sporadic sleep schedule. It can’t be healthy for them.

This week I bought two assassin snails to help eradicate the growing snail population in my fish tank. It’s a 35-gallon tank currently home to a Red-Tailed Shark, 2 Mollies, a White Skirt Tetra, and an Albino Pleco.

Just finished rewatching Trailer Park Boys. Now I’m watching It’s Always Sunny again. Patiently awaiting the new Mick Jenkins album. It can’t come out soon enough. Drink more water.
