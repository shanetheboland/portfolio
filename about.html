About Me

I was born and raised in Upstate New York. I live in a small house in the woods and I make things for the Internet. It’s quiet here and I never really have to wear pants.

I make my living as a front-end web designer. I design and code the parts of the Internet that you see and interact with.

I prefer to work fast and make mistakes. I like to get projects out into the world as soon as possible. Even if it’s ugly, unfinished, and missing a few pieces. Get it out into the world, collect feedback, make improvements, repeat.

I believe the Internet should be fast, inexpensive, and accessible to all. To help out with that cause, I strive to build websites that are fast, responsive, accessible, and content-focused.
