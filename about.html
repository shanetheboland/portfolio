About

I was born and raised in upstate New York. I live in a small house in the woods and I make things for the Internet.
It’s quiet here and for that I am grateful.

I am a front-end web designer. This means I build the parts of the Internet that you see and interact with.

The Internet is a place meant for open and honest communication between all people. So I strive to build websites that are fast, responsive, accessible, and content-focused.
