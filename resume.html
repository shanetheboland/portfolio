The Résumé of Shane Boland

I started freelancing six years ago and I’ve handled a lot of web projects from start to finish. I take my projects seriously and I have a real passion for web design.

I believe the Internet should be fast, inexpensive, and accessible to all.

To help out with that cause, I strive to build websites that are fast, responsive, accessible, and content-focused.

I love learning about new technologies. Below are some of the technologies I like to work with the most.

Favorite Technologies

  • Design
    Photoshop, Illustrator, Pen & Paper
  • Web
    HTML, Jekyll, WordPress, Tumblr, Shopify
  • CSS
    PostCSS, SCSS
  • JavaScript
    jQuery, React, NPM, Grunt, Gulp, Webpack
  • Other
    Git, Sublime Text, Chrome Dev Tools, imageOptim, Raspberry Pi

I prefer to work fast and make mistakes. I like to get projects out into the world as soon as possible. Even if it’s ugly, unfinished, and missing a few pieces. Get it out into the world, collect feedback, make improvements, repeat.

Below are some of the skills I bring to the table.

Skills

  • Design

  • Soft Skills
    Problem Solving, Forgetting to Eat Lunch, Swearing at Computer, Staying up ’til 6 in the morning.
